How it works & where the numbers come from

The panel shows the same Macrobias fundamental bias you see on the dashboard — built from rate differentials, central-bank stance, 10-year yield spreads, COT positioning and risk sentiment. The extension pulls it live from the Macrobias feed and refreshes every minute, following whatever pair you switch to.

It's an analytical aid, not financial advice or a buy/sell signal. Pair the bias with your own technical entry — see the methodology for exactly how the score is built.
