Common questions

Is this financial advice?

No. Macrobias is an analytical aid that shows the fundamental bias — you make your own decisions. See our risk disclaimer.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel in one click from your account. You keep access until the end of the billing period.

Is the data live?

Pro updates continuously from our fundamental feeds. Free tier sees daily-delayed scores on the majors only.

Does it work with my broker?

Macrobias is broker-agnostic — it's the fundamental layer beside your chart. The indicator drops onto any TradingView chart.
